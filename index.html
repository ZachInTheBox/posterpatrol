Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LA rental compliance

Los Angeles Municipal Code sections 165.05 and 151.09 require specific tenant rights and housing compliance posters to be displayed in rental properties. These include tenant rights notifications, rent stabilization information, and housing quality standards.

LA housing regulations can change multiple times per year. Our subscription service monitors these changes continuously and automatically sends you updated posters whenever requirements change, ensuring you stay compliant without having to track regulations yourself.

Non-compliance can result in fines from the city, legal issues with tenants, and complications during property inspections. Staying compliant protects you from penalties and demonstrates good faith compliance with tenant protection laws.
